A History Of My Life Through Sports

Growing up, the priorities of my life involved school and sports, with the latter seemingly being the only thing bringing me daily joy.

I played competitive basketball and soccer from elementary school to high school, but that came with its challenges.

3/17/20

A 2 year recovery began just as COVID did, and my somewhat new life had come to a start. March 17, 2020, was effectively the end of my competitive basketball and soccer career. I spent the year sitting around watching movies and learning/practicing walking again.

The recovery lead to a handful of new interests and priorities in my life (see the Interests page). Walking became a habit. I picked up a new sport in tennis and made the varsity team when I tried out senior year. All that time I had siting in my room during COVID watching movies spiked my interest in them, which is why I'm trying to learn more about them in the film studies program here.

Lake Oswego High School Tennis Team Seniors, 2022

Now, I try to prioritize my physical and mental health by spending free time in the gym or doing anything active that I can do!
